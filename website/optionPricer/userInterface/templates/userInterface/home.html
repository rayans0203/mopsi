How does it work ?


This website was built by three undeergraduate students from the École Nationale des Ponts et Chaussées (ENPC) in order to price options given a set of parameters, and the model in which the user wants to price the option.

The outputs are the following:
- price of the option;
- computation time;
- the bounds of the confidence interval, considering the precision input.

If you wish to have any additional information what concerning our computation method, etc... Please click here to contact us.
